Personalised education for every child. Strengths-first, individually paced, never one-size-fits-all.

The Lodestone Centre offers personalised, strengths-first support for children with ADHD, dyscalculia, dyslexia and other learning differences in Subang Jaya, Petaling Jaya and the Klang Valley, including dysgraphia, autism, speech delay and slower learning, as well as gifted children and those who simply learn at their own pace. Every program is built around the individual child. The Lodestone Centre is an inclusive provider of non-curricular teaching, personal development training, motivational courses, educational support workshops and character development workshops. This free check and our screening and programs are designed to support learning and growth, and are not a substitute for formal schooling or clinical diagnosis.
