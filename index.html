About the centre

A space where every child is met where they are. Neurodivergent, gifted, or simply unique.

Every child learns differently. That's not a problem to fix, it's our entire premise. We design programs around your child's strengths, not a label. Whether your child has dyslexia, ADHD, or autism; whether they're ahead and bored in school; or whether they simply learn at their own pace, we start with who they are.

Who Lodestone is for

Children with dyslexia, ADHD, or autism. Gifted children who need more challenge. Children who don't fit neatly into either box. And children who just want a place where being themselves is enough.

We specialise in personalisation. That's it.

Our promise to your family

Children come to us when school feels too hard, too loud, or too fast. They leave knowing how they learn, what they're great at, and that being different is a strength worth celebrating.

Etiquette · Virtue · Order

Japanese-inspired foundations
Every session is grounded in the same principles that shape our Lodestone Evo program, clean habits, kind hearts, quiet order. Children learn how to be, not just what to do.

Evidence-based methods

Specialist-trained
Orton-Gillingham–informed literacy, multi-sensory math, neuroaffirming SEL, each taught by educators trained in that specific method, never improvised.
